# Experiment Plan: BOND-GCL

**Problem**：false-negative repeated repulsion in graph contrastive learning for node classification  
**Method Thesis**：Basin-level negative mass aggregation reduces repeated repulsion from anchor-local semantic basins without collapsing denominator diversity.  
**Date**：2026-06-26  
**Stage**：smoke/pilot planning only

## Claim Map

| Claim | Why It Matters | Minimum Convincing Evidence | Linked Blocks |
|---|---|---|---|
| C1: repeated basin repulsion exists | 区分 BOND 与 ordinary pair false-negative scoring | label-only offline false-negative mass exposure shows over-counted same-class/basin repulsion in GRACE | B1 |
| C2: basin aggregation is not pair reweighting | 保护新颖性 | same pair weights / shuffled basin controls are weaker than BOND | B2 |
| C3: BOND is not E2Neg-style small negative sampling | 保护新颖性 | BOND full-denominator aggregation beats or differs diagnostically from center/small-negative controls | B3 |
| C4: node classification utility | 决定是否继续 | frozen encoder + Logistic Regression not below GRACE and improves on at least 2/3 pilot citation datasets | B4 |

## Paper Storyline

- Main paper must prove：BOND addresses repeated basin-level repulsive mass, not just pair reweighting or smaller denominator。
- Appendix can support：cap sensitivity, basin constructor sensitivity, Amazon/Photo stability。
- Experiments intentionally cut：KAN encoder, LLM semantic teacher, synthetic hard negative generation。

## Experiment Blocks

### Block 1: Cora Seed-0 Smoke

- Claim tested：C1 / implementation sanity。
- Dataset / split / task：Cora, stratified random `1:1:8`, seed 0, node classification。
- Compared systems：GRACE original, BOND-cap-only。
- Metrics：LogReg val/test@best, training loss, denominator effective number, uniformity, embedding rank, offline false-negative mass exposure。
- Success criterion：runs without NaN; BOND does not collapse; exposure decreases。
- Failure interpretation：if unstable or uniformity collapses, revise loss before pilot。
- Priority：MUST-RUN。

### Block 2: Reweighting Counterfactual

- Claim tested：C2。
- Compared systems：BOND-cap-only, same pair weights / shuffled basins, random basin same size histogram, ProGCL-style proxy。
- Metrics：LogReg test@best, exposure, denominator effective number。
- Success criterion：BOND beats shuffled/random controls; otherwise mechanism is not isolated。
- Priority：MUST-RUN。

### Block 3: Sampling Counterfactual

- Claim tested：C3。
- Compared systems：E2Neg-style center/small negatives, random small negatives, hardest small negatives, BOND-cap-only。
- Metrics：accuracy, exposure, selected/capped mass distribution, uniformity。
- Success criterion：BOND not explained by smaller denominator or center sampling。
- Priority：MUST-RUN。

### Block 4: Citation Pilot

- Claim tested：C4。
- Dataset：Cora/CiteSeer/PubMed, seeds 0-4, `1:1:8`。
- Compared systems：GRACE, best BOND from smoke, strongest counterfactual from B2/B3。
- Success criterion：BOND improves or ties GRACE on at least 2/3 datasets while diagnostics support C1-C3。
- Priority：MUST-RUN only if B1-B3 pass。

### Block 5: Stability / Scope Diagnostic

- Dataset：DBLP, Computers, Photo after citation pilot passes。
- Purpose：check homophily / feature-noise dependence。
- Priority：NICE-TO-HAVE before formal。

## Compute and Data Budget

- Smoke total：约 2-6 GPU-hours。
- Pilot total：约 8-20 GPU-hours。
- Data preparation：reuse existing splits; no new labels。
- Biggest bottleneck：efficient basin construction and reproducible diagnostics。

## Risks and Mitigations

- **Risk**：BOND 等价 pair reweighting。  
  **Mitigation**：same pair weight / shuffled basin control。

- **Risk**：BOND 只是 smaller denominator。  
  **Mitigation**：denominator effective number and E2Neg-style controls。

- **Risk**：Basin constructor 是真正贡献。  
  **Mitigation**：random basin same histogram and single-signal ablation。

- **Risk**：只在同配 citation graphs 有效。  
  **Mitigation**：after citation pilot, test DBLP/Computers/Photo as development。
