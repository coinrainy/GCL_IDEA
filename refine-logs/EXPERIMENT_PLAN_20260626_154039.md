## GO / KILL Rules

### GO to SP-M1 pilot

All must hold:

1. S2 is at least competitive with S0/S1 on Cora seed0 smoke.
2. S2 improves boundary energy retention or effective rank over S1.
3. S5 random-spectrum guard does not match S2.
4. S3 no-boundary-energy loses the key mechanism diagnostic.

### REVISE

- S2 improves boundary/rank diagnostics but not accuracy：revise objective, not threshold/weight tuning.
- S2 only beats S1 because of parameter budget：add matched controls before any pilot.

### KILL

- S1 >= S2 and S5 ~= S2：kill boundary-energy story.
- S0 >> all negative-free variants：pivot away from negative-free boundary preservation.
- Diagnostics improve but B5-style no accuracy conversion repeats：do not continue by tuning diagnostics.
