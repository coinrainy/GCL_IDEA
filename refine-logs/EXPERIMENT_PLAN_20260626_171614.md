- `O14 <= max(O0..O7, O13)`：kill。
- `O10`, `O11`, `O12`, or `O9` within 0.3 absolute accuracy points of O14：kill as shortcut/artifact.
- held-out operator response prediction no better than random/shuffled：kill.
- O14 improves response metrics but not `test_acc_at_best_val` over matched controls：kill.
- O14 only wins when using more epochs/params/search budget：kill.
